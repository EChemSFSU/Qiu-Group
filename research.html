Understanding the charge transfer at the interface of electrode and molecules/solids

 

 

 

Our research focuses on understanding the charge transfer at the interface of electrode and solids/molecules in the (photo)electrocatalytic anodic reactions.

 

(1) Plasmonic metal nanoparticle as anodes

 

  • Investigating the (photo)electrocatalytic water oxidation activities with plasmonic gold nanoparticle electrode with Ni-, Co- and Fe-based electrocatalysts;
  • Conducting operando electrochemical (EC)-AFM and SERS studies of the electrocatalysts.

 

(2) Electrooxidation of alcohol molecules

 

  • Understanding the impact of molecular structures of alcohol molecules on the electrooxidation efficiency

 

(3) Oxygen evoluation reaction (OER) in seawater

 

  • Measuring the stability and efficiency of electrocatalysts for OER in seawater
