PI

Dr. Jingjing Qiu

Dr. Jingjing Qiu got her bachelor degree in Chemical Engineering with a minor in Mathematical Finance from Xiamen University in 2010.  She did her research on surface-plasmon-mediated surface chemistry and achieved her Ph.D. under the guidance of Professor David Wei at University of Florida in 2015. Following that, she moved from east coast to west coast and conducted her postdoctoral research on semiconductor and catalyst  interface  in photoelectrochemical (PEC) water oxidation in University of Oregon with Professor Shannon Boettcher. Dr. Qiu joined the Department of Chemistry and Biochemistry of San Francisco State University in August 2018. Her research group is located in TH112 at SF State.
